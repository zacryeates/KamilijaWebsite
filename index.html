S H O R T   F I L M S

"Egg Shell"
poetic film (2020)

A woman travels far to meet a man who can help her find inspiration. What does a person do when they cannot find it? Should they worry? Can they ask for help? The egg has the answer.

"Words for Breakfast"
mockumentary (2019)

A short mockumentary commercial about the mainstream media and its impact on people.

"Song of Myself"
short fiction film (2018)

A multi-timeline and multi-dimension film about lost love, fear and power of the mind.

"Letter to the Idol"
short fiction film (2019)

In a society where everyone is being watched through blue screens, human connection, love and compassion have disappeared. Writer Matthew who is spreading eternal values is defamed by the media. His most loyal reader Maria writes him a letter, having no idea what an important mission awaits her.

"Shadow of the Sun"
s hort fiction fillm (2019)

Janus lives in a world where there is​ no darkness but the fear of it is chasing him in his recurring dreams about night. One morning he wakes up holding a sun-shaped ring from his dream. The dreams and fears start to enter his reality when shortly he awakes in a parallel world where the dark is reigning.

V I D E O   A R T

Nature is known to represent freedom, exploration and the natural start of everything. Man-made parks, beaches, ponds are supposed to mirror the experience of nature, however, because they are in confinements of the city and its busy noisy landscape, they give the sensation of false and distorted freedom of nature.

"Caged Freedom"
video art/soundsape piece (2020)

It is a series of 4 soundscapes which was part of the Eufonia Festival 2020. The rest of the soundscapes are available  here .

"Reflected Truth"
video art (20​20)

This piece was produced by an online artist platfrom Vibe. It reflects on the mystical and beautiful connection between us humans and nature showing ​how, despite all the differences and borders, similar we are.

"Interwater"
mood piece (2020)

This piece was also created for the platfrom Vibe and tells a story about two lovers living in paralel universes who can only connect throught the water.

"Conte d'automne"
fashion film (2021)

This fashion film was a collaboration with a stylist/art director Frida Chen and brands BRACHMANN and Nobi Talai

A B O U T

         Kamilija Tekle Cizaite was born in Vilnius, Lithuania in 1998. She is a filmmaker, photographer and musician based in Berlin. In 2017 she graduated from National M. K. Ciurlionis School of Arts where she studied classical flute and piano for 12 years.
         Then she studied jazz flute in Lithuanian Academy of Music and Theatre for one year where she also took classes of screenwriting and directing.

          Since September 2018 she is studying at Catalyst Institute For Creative Arts and Technology in Berlin, Germany where she has participated in many creative projects as a writer, director, sound and video editor, director of photography, producer, set designer.
          Her short movies have successfully participated in festivals in Germany, Lithuania and the United States.
          Kamilija Tekle is always open for new creative and commercial projects, collaborations and explorations of different styles and ideas.
